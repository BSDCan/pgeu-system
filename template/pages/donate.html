Donate

PostgreSQL Europe appreciates all donations from individuals and companies in our outside of Europe. Large or small, everything is appreciated. For those who wish to pay a bit more and get some more in return, we also have sponsorship opportunities.

If you wish to be listed amongst our official donors, please indicate so in the freetext field when you make a donation. Unless you explicitly ask us to, we will not list the personal or company name who makes a donation.

What will my donation be used for?

All donations received by PostgreSQL Europe will be used to help promote PostgreSQL in and around Europe. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Promotion material (flyers, folders etc)
  • Merchandise for events (t-shirts, elephants, pins etc)
  • Arranging the PGDay.EU European PostgreSQL conference
  • Assisting local PostgreSQL events and conferences

Donate now!

PostgreSQL Europe is a registered non-profit organisation in France. This may mean your donation is tax-deductible in your country.
