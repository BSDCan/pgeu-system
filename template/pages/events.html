Events

PostgreSQL Europe is currently involved in organising presence for the following events. We also recommend you check out the global community list of events and the Wiki site with upcoming events!

FOSDEM 2009

Location: Brussels, Belguim
Date: 7-8 February 2009

PostgreSQL Europe will have a booth at FOSDEM 2009. We will also share a devroom with the BSD groups, where there will be a full track of talks. For up-to-date details, please see the page on the wiki.

The Call for Papers is out - please submit your talk now! For details, see the wiki.

Prague PostgreSQL Developers Day 2009

Location: Prague, Czech Republic
Date: 12 Frebruary 2009

Prague PostgreSQL Developers' Day is one day event focused on PostgreSQL database application developers and database administrators.

Most of presentation will be in Czech or Slovak language. More information is available on the website.

Chemnitzer Linuxtage 2009

Location: Chemnitz, Germany
Date: 14-15 March 2009

PostgreSQL Europe plans a presence at CLT 2009 with a booth and a workshop. Further details will be announced later.

PGDay.EU 2009

Location: Paris, France
Date: To be determined

This is the largest European PostgreSQL conference of 2009. This year, it will be held in cooperation with PostgreSQLFr. Further details will be announced later.
